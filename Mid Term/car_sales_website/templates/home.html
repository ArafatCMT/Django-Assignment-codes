Audi’s small utility vehicle is a smart choice, as evidenced by the Best Buy award it received from The Car Guide for 2021. There’s only one engine on the menu, but it’s a good one, namely a turbocharged 2.0-litre four-cylinder that delivers 228 horsepower and 258 pound-feet of torque. More powerful models are sold in Europe, including the RS Q3, but sadly not in North America.
The 2021 Audi Q3 is a well-rounded subcompact SUV that delivers the refinement and luxury of a larger model in a smaller package. It’s stable and poised, and it offers good comfort in the front or rear seats. Gas mileage, however, is disappointing, and its ride is firm with the available 19-inch wheels.
